# Opening Screen

## Study Title

Expert Interaction Evaluation Study

## Introduction

This study evaluates how communication style influences interactions between users and experts in digital support systems.

You will be presented with several short scenarios showing conversations between a user and a university expert. Your task is to evaluate how helpful and trustworthy the expert responses appear.

The survey contains 4 scenarios and takes approximately 8–10 minutes to complete.

Your responses are anonymous and will be used exclusively for academic research conducted as part of a doctoral dissertation.

Please read each interaction carefully before answering the evaluation questions.

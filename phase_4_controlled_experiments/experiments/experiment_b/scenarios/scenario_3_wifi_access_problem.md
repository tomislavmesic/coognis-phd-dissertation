# Wi-Fi Access Problem

## Scenario Context

You, as a user, try to connect your laptop to the university Wi-Fi network, but the connection fails.
Because internet access is required for your work, you contact the university support system.
The system connects you with an IT support expert.

## Core Facts

- Check Wi-Fi is enabled
- Select university network
- Enter login credentials
- Restart device if needed
- Contact IT support if issue continues

## Matched Interaction

Each participant evaluates the matched condition corresponding to their selected communication persona.

### STJ User -> STJ Expert

> **User:**
> I cannot connect to the university Wi-Fi. What should I do?

> **Expert:**
> Follow the standard connection procedure.
>
> Step 1: Check that Wi-Fi is enabled on your device.
> Step 2: Select the university network from the list of available networks.

> **User:**
> How do I reconnect to the network?

> **Expert:**
> Step 3: Enter your university login credentials to connect to the network.

> **User:**
> What if it still does not work?

> **Expert:**
> Step 4: Restart your device and attempt the connection again.

> **User:**
> Who should I contact if the problem continues?

> **Expert:**
> Step 5: Contact IT support if the issue continues after completing these steps.

> **User:**
> I cannot connect to the university Wi-Fi. What should I do?

> **Expert:**
> Wi-Fi connection failures usually occur because the device cannot properly detect or authenticate to the network.
> To resolve this, first verify that Wi-Fi is enabled and then select the university network so your device can attempt connection.

> **User:**
> How do I reconnect to the network?

> **Expert:**
> Entering your university login credentials allows the system to authenticate your device and grant access.

> **User:**
> What if it still does not work?

> **Expert:**
> Restarting the device resets temporary network states and often resolves connection errors.

> **User:**
> Who should I contact if the problem continues?

> **Expert:**
> If the issue persists after these steps, IT support can analyze the connection problem directly.

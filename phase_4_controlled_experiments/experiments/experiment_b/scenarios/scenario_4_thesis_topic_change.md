# Thesis Topic Change

## Scenario Context

You, as a user, are working on your thesis but realize that another topic may better match your research interests.
Because you are unsure whether changing the topic is allowed, you contact the university support system.
The system connects you with an academic advisor.

## Core Facts

- Contact thesis supervisor
- Submit topic change request
- Provide justification for change
- Wait for approval
- Follow updated thesis plan

## Matched Interaction

Each participant evaluates the matched condition corresponding to their selected communication persona.

### STJ User -> STJ Expert

> **User:**
> I am thinking about changing my thesis topic. What should I do?

> **Expert:**
> Follow the official thesis topic change procedure.
>
> Step 1: Contact your thesis supervisor to discuss the change.
> Step 2: Submit a formal topic change request.

> **User:**
> What do I need to include in the request?

> **Expert:**
> Step 3: Provide justification explaining the reason for the topic change.

> **User:**
> What happens after I submit it?

> **Expert:**
> Step 4: Wait while the supervisor reviews and approves the request.

> **User:**
> What should I do after approval?

> **Expert:**
> Step 5: Follow the updated thesis plan once approval is granted.

# Opening Screen

## Study Title

Evaluation of AI-Based Support Responses

## Introduction

This study evaluates how different formats of information delivery influence user experience in digital support systems.

You will be presented with 6 short scenarios, each followed by two alternative responses generated by a support system. Your task is to evaluate both responses based on clarity, usefulness, and trust.

The survey is anonymous and takes approximately 12–15 minutes to complete.

The collected responses will be used exclusively for academic research within a doctoral dissertation.

# Wi-Fi Access Problem

## Scenario Context

You are trying to connect your laptop to the university Wi-Fi network on campus, but the connection repeatedly fails. Since you cannot access the internet, you ask the university IT support system for help.

## User Prompt

> My laptop cannot connect to the university Wi-Fi. It keeps rejecting my password.

## Generic Response

When a device cannot connect to the university Wi-Fi network, the issue is often related to authentication or stored network settings. It is recommended to verify that the username and password are correct and to reset the password if necessary. In some cases, removing the saved network configuration and reconnecting to the network may resolve the issue. If the problem persists, contacting IT support is advised.

## Adapted Responses

### STJ

If you cannot connect to the university Wi-Fi network, follow the standard troubleshooting procedure step by step.
1. Check that your username and password are correct.
2. Reset your password if necessary.
3. Remove the saved Wi-Fi network from your device.
4. Reconnect to the network and enter your credentials again.
5. If the problem continues, contact IT support.

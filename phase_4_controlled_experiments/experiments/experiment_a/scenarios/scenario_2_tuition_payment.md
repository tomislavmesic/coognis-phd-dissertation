# Tuition Payment Delay

## Scenario Context

Your tuition payment will be late because of a banking issue.
You want to know what you should do.

## User Prompt

> My tuition payment will be late due to banking issues. Will I be penalized?

## Generic Response

If a student expects difficulty paying tuition by the required deadline, the recommended approach is to contact the university finance office. In some situations, students may request an extension of the payment deadline or apply for an installment arrangement. Such requests normally need to be submitted before the official payment deadline. After the request is received, the finance office reviews the situation and informs the student about the decision.

## Adapted Responses

### STJ

If you may not be able to pay the tuition fee on time, the university provides a clear procedure for handling this situation.
 
Follow these steps:
1. Contact the university finance office as soon as possible.
2. Ask whether you can request a payment extension or an installment plan.
3. Submit the request before the official payment deadline.
4. Wait for the finance office to review your request and inform you about the decision.

# Student Exchange Application

## Scenario Context

You want to apply for an Erasmus exchange program next semester.
You ask the university support system how to start the application process.

## User Prompt

> I would like to apply for Erasmus next semester. What should I do and what are the deadlines?

## Generic Response

Applications for student exchange programs are usually completed through a defined administrative process. Students are expected to verify eligibility requirements and prepare the necessary documentation before submitting the application through the university portal. The submission is reviewed by the international office, after which a decision is communicated. If approval is granted, confirmation of participation is required.

## Adapted Responses

### STJ

If you want to apply for the student exchange program, follow the standard application procedure used by the university.
1. Check that you meet the eligibility requirements.
2. Prepare the required documents.
3. Submit the application through the university portal.
4. Wait for the international office to review the application.
5. If your application is approved, confirm your participation.

### NTP

The student exchange application follows a structured administrative workflow.

Eligibility requirements determine whether the student can participate, so the first step is to verify those conditions. After eligibility is confirmed, the required documents must be prepared and submitted through the university portal. The international office then evaluates the submission and makes a decision. If the outcome is positive, the student confirms participation.

### NFJ

Applying for an exchange program can feel complex at first, but the process becomes clear once the main steps are understood.

Start by checking that you meet the eligibility requirements. Then prepare the required documents and submit your application through the university portal. The international office will review your submission and inform you about the result. If your application is approved, you confirm your participation.

### SFP

- Check eligibility requirements.
- Prepare required documents.
- Submit the application through the university portal.
- Wait for the international office to review the application.
- Confirm participation if approved.
